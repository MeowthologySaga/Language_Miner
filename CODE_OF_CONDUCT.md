# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Language Miner community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people;
- respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences;
- giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback;
- accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes;
- focusing on what is best for the community, not just for us as individuals.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind;
- trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks;
- public or private harassment;
- publishing another person’s private information without explicit permission;
- distributing malware, credential-stealing content, or intentionally harmful UGC;
- other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

The official Language Miner Discord is a general-audience space. Content that is pornographic, graphically violent, illegal, exploitative, or designed to evade safety review is not permitted there.

## Enforcement responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, UGC links, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and when an individual officially represents the project in public spaces. External UGC is not automatically an official project space, but behavior that targets this community may still affect participation privileges.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately through GitHub’s repository moderation tools or the private moderator channel announced in the official community. Do not use the security advisory form for ordinary conduct disputes. Do not include unnecessary personal data or publish a report as a public issue.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly on a best-effort basis. Community leaders will respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

## Enforcement guidelines

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining consequences:

### 1. Correction

**Community impact:** Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome.

**Consequence:** A private, written warning providing clarity around the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning

**Community impact:** A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

**Consequence:** A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved for a specified period, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct. Violating these terms may lead to a temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. Temporary ban

**Community impact:** A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence:** A temporary ban from interaction or public communication with the community. Violating these terms may lead to a permanent ban.

### 4. Permanent ban

**Community impact:** A pattern of violating community standards, sustained harassment, aggression toward individuals, or disparagement of classes of individuals.

**Consequence:** A permanent ban from public interaction within the community.
