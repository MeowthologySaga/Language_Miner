01 · THE GAP

Why can a familiar word still disappear when you need to speak?

Recognizing a meaning and retrieving a sentence are different skills. Language Miner does not let study end at “I saw it.”

Isolated words

The meaning remains, but the sentence falls apart

You lose which verb and preposition fit together, what tone sounds natural, and when a phrase is used.

Sentences with context

Vocabulary, grammar, and situation stay connected

A real sentence you chose gives you a complete unit to retrieve in the next conversation.

02–03 · BALANCE

Discover through input. Make it yours through output.

Connect reading and listening to speaking and writing through the same sentence.

Read & Listen · Input

Build recognition of natural English

Find expressions you actually want in PDFs, websites, audio, and video. Reading and listening cards make form and sound familiar.

Speak & Write · Output

Build retrieval at the moment of need

Use speaking cards, writing practice, and Character Chat to produce English from a meaning or situation. A mistake becomes a hint for the next review.

Spend study-earned diamonds in official games such as Abyss Summoner, Drillheart Defense, and Cat Odyssey, or in validated user-made packs. Packs run in isolation after technical checks, and network access is denied by default.

06 · FIRST START

A ten-minute starting sequence with no AI setup

AI starts disconnected. Experience cards and review first, then connect only what you need.

  1. Open the built-in practice text in Web Reader.
    Practice a real card save from the fixed sentence without contacting an external website.
  2. Check the reading card's sentence, meaning, and context.
    Edit them to match the situation you want to remember.
  3. Save and complete the first review.
    Try retrieval before choosing Show answer.
  4. Retrieve it in a short writing exercise.
    Produce the English sentence from the same meaning.
  5. Connect AI only if needed.
    Consider local Ollama first; review data and cost before cloud use.
07 · YOUR BOUNDARY

Your data, your key, your choice of connection.

The app explains real storage, transfer, and cost boundaries instead of using “local” or “free” as vague promises.

  • Local storageCards and conversations stay on this PC by default. Learning data other than API keys may be plaintext.
  • Disconnected by defaultNo Gemini or Google AI request happens before consent. Only loopback Ollama is labeled local.
  • BYOK cloudUse your key and review provider, content, calls, and cost range before the task.
  • Limits of cost guardsThe app shows an on-device estimate. Provider quota and budget alerts are still needed.
  • UGC runtime limitsHashes, permissions, CSP, and archive structure are checked; packs that are not ready cannot run.
  • No central trackingThe project runs no advertising, analytics, or developer telemetry server.
